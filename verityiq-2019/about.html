About Us

We are a team of talented professionals who share a passion for providing technology solutions to educational institutions.

Developed by higher education professionals in partnership with higher education institutions, Verity addresses the challenges of the Eduscape ~ declining enrollments, rising costs, and operational redundancies.

Our mission is to streamline the communication efforts across the entire student's lifecycle for better prospect and student retention, conversion, and placement.

Our Story

How it all started...



We have witnessed the inneficiencies caused by school departments operating in silos. We grew weary of cumbersome excel spreadsheets to manage performance data, and have seen low conversion rates due to the lack of follow-up automation.

We realized we had the knowledge and talent to do more! As a team, we have over 50 years of combined experience in higher education admissions, marketing, recruitment, and education technology. We partner with the best out there to distinguish ourselves from legacy and embrace the modern.

Verity brings campus operations back to one single system to deliver one version of the truth. Data is collected, analyzed, and acted upon from within the same platform, eliminating the need to manually aggregate information from various systems, thus eliminating man hours and increasing the accuracy of the reported results.

In simple words, Verity collects, reports, and acts on your data, as it happens.

Our CRM solution is affordable and flexible to meet the needs of traditional and non-traditional institutions, OPMS, foundations, including career colleges and the call centers that serve them. The result is a more significant ROI on lead spend, higher conversions, greater student engagement and, ultimately, higher retention.
