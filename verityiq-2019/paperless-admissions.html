Enrollment Workflows

Verity enables flexible configuration for enrollment and signature workflows. Surveys and final documents are easily built and then delivered electronically to applicants with easy to follow requirements for review, acknowledgement, and signature. Admissions staff monitor the progress of each application at every step to ensure all the requirements are met. The signed documents are then stored and easily accessed from document hubs.

Applicant Portal

Verity offers the institution an intuitive, mobile-first designed portal for applicants to initiate and complete their enrollment online. The applicants are prompted with simple surveys meant to collect the required information, and then presented with documents to review, acknowlege, and e-sign. If the enrollment workflow requires an application fee or deposit, the applicant would easily make the payment online via different payment methods.
